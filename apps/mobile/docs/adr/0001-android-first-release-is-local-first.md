# Make the first Android release local-first

The first production Android release will provide local feature parity with the iOS practice experience, including practice modes, review, history, on-device analysis, reminders, and local persistence. Phones and tablets in portrait and landscape are release-supported; foldables and ChromeOS receive basic compatibility, while other Android form factors are excluded. Production native builds support the 64-bit `arm64-v8a` and `x86_64` ABIs; 32-bit-only devices are excluded even when they meet the Android 7.0 minimum OS version. Cross-platform progress synchronization is excluded so Android native parity and Google Play readiness can be delivered without coupling the effort to a new identity and cloud-backend design; that capability remains a separate future product decision.
